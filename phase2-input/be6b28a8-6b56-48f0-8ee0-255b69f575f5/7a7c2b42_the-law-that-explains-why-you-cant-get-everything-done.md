# The 'law' that explains why you can't get everything done⏱🚫

## NotebookLM-extracted transcript (Google's ASR + indexing)

Parkinson's law is the old adage that says that work expands to fill the time allotted for its completion have a month to get something done well it'll likely take that whole month have a week probably going to take a week have a day an hour just a few minutes well might not be pretty but the odds are good that it's going to get done setting time limits on your work sessions creates a sense of urgency which helps you to focus and work more efficiently
