# This Simple Trick Makes People Say YES Way More!

## NotebookLM-extracted transcript (Google's ASR + indexing)

here's a crazy stat from Stanford people are 50% more likely to say yes to a big request if they've already said yes to a small one first it's called the foot in the door technique and it's been a psychological gold mine since the 1960s the idea is simple if you want someone to buy don't start with a sales pitch start with a tiny favor something frictionless effortless like clicking a button or answering a simple question or saying yes to a basic poll because once someone agrees to anything even something meaningless they're psychologically more likely to keep saying yes in order to stay consistent think of it like pushing a heavy shopping cart that first push is tough but once it's rolling easy and this works across funnels sales pages and even cold outreach so try this instead of jumping straight into your offer start your funnel or your ad with a micro ask first something like "Do you struggle with X?" Or "Curious if this could work for you click here." Or "Quick question yes or no." That tiny action creates momentum it shifts the user from passive observer to active participant and once they've taken one step they're far more likely to take the next so if your funnel's stalling at the very start stop asking for the sale start by asking for something
