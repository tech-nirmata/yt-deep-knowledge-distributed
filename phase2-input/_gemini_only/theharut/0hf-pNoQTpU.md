- **Top Level**: "Video Uploaded" label pointing downward to a line of four stick figures representing a small initial test audience.
- **Metrics Split**: The flow splits through a vertical dotted divider showing low-tier initial view counts (e.g., 1.9k).
- **Secondary Level**: Downward arrows lead to a wider row of eight stick figures representing expanded distribution.
