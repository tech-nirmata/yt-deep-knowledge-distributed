## Visual frameworks

### The "SVS" Method Checklist
A text-based strategic pipeline presented in Google Docs containing the following sequential criteria:
1. **Niche selection**: Identifying a niche of personal interest that is actively growing.
2. **Content team assembly**: Hiring specialized contractors (writers, editors, voiceover artists).
3. **Competitor modeling**: Isolating successful small competitors.
4. **Market research**: Continuous monitoring of high-performing video trends.
5. **Doubling down**: Creating variations of topics that perform best on your own channel.
6. **Progressive refinement**: Iterating video ideas and formats daily.
7. **Action/Result mindset**: Executing aggressively while remaining patient for analytical feedback.
