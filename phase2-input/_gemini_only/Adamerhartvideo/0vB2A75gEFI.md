## Visual frameworks
The video structure is built around five minimalist title card dividers:
1. **Level 1: First** (Entering an uncontested market)
2. **Level 2: Different** (Standing out as competitors enter)
3. **Level 3: Unique** (Introducing a unique mechanism to a crowded market)
4. **Level 4: Improved** (Amplifying and optimizing the unique mechanism)
5. **Level 5: Re-Invent** (Focusing on brand, experience, and community in a saturated market)
