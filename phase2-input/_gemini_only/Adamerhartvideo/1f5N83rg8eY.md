## Visual frameworks

### The Psychology Gap
A dual-column table used to contrast customer verbalizations with behavioral data.
* **Left Column:** "What They Say They Want" (associated with features, low cost, full comprehensive solutions, and immediate buying readiness).
* **Right Column:** "What They Actually Buy" (associated with business strategy, band-aid quick fixes, and risk-reversal confirmation).

### The Master Spreadsheet Template
A linear grid designed to compile behavioral variables for qualitative analysis.
* **Columns:** Name, Source, Contact Date, Questions, Content Consumed, Time to Purchase, Purchase Amount, Status, Notes.

## Visual storytelling devices

* **Marker Drawing Cutaways:** The speaker interacts directly with physical diagrams on paper using bright colored markers to underline key terms, draw connectors, or segment concepts.
