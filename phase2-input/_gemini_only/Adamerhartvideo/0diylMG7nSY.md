* **Top Level (Light Grey):** Labeled "TRAFFIC". Represents the initial stage of customer attraction.
* **Middle Level (Medium Blue):** Labeled "ENGAGEMENT". Represents the nurturing and relationship-building stage.
* **Bottom Level (Dark Blue):** Labeled "CONVERSION". Represents the final sales action.

## Visual storytelling devices

* **Multicam Angle Shifts:** Transitions from direct frontal camera to a 45-degree profile view to separate conversational talk from structured lists.
* **Color Temperature Shifts:** Changing the background room lights from blue to red when addressing marketing errors.
* **Stylistic Color Filters:** Turning the camera feed black-and-white to highlight dramatic self-mockery or historic/flashback segments.
* **Visual Humour Inserts:** Placing the speaker's face onto low-budget green-screen backgrounds (playing ukulele, sitting on cash piles, acting as a stereotypical salesman).
