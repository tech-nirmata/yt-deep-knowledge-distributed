## Visual frameworks

* **S**egmentation: Breaking down the market into smaller, manageable groups.
* **T**argeting: Identifying and selecting specific segments to focus on.
* **P**ositioning: Defining how the brand/product is perceived relative to competitors.
* **D**ifferentiation: Highlighting unique features and benefits that set the offering apart.

**Customer Journey Stages:**
* **Awareness:** Potential customers become aware of the brand/product.
* **Interest:** Customers show interest and seek more information.
* **Decision:** Customers evaluate options and decide to purchase.
* **Action:** Customers make a purchase.
* **Loyalty:** Customers become repeat buyers and advocates.
