- **Color styling:** Deep blacks and high-contrast yellow/gold graphics create a premium, high-energy aesthetic.
- **B-roll treatments:** External footage is desaturated, highly stylized, or presented in monochrome sketch filters to maintain a cohesive, dark, and analytical visual tone.
