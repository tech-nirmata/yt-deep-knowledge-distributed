## Visual storytelling devices

- **Pattern Interrupts**: Fast transition cuts (00:36) break up the talking-head structure to maintain high viewer attention.
- **Dynamic Text Elements**: Key vocabulary and marketing metrics drop directly into the frame beside the presenter's head to visually anchor abstract strategies.
- **Color Coding**: Financial charts change color from red (losses) to blue/green (success) to instantly communicate the concept of business growth.
- **Digital Walkthroughs**: Transitioning from a direct camera feed to clean, full-screen website screenshots lets the viewer study layout patterns directly.
