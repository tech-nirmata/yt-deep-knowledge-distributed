## Visual frameworks
- **The 7-Step Content Marketing System:** A linear process framework represented throughout the video by a dark gray lower-third tab containing bold blue numbers and white capital letters:
  - Step 1: **FIND FILTER** (evaluated using a two-branch split: A. Is it useful / B. How to structure).
  - Step 2: **CONSUME** (focuses on input processing).
  - Step 3: **FOCUS FILTER** (focuses on narrowing many ideas down to a single theme).
  - Step 4: **CREATE** (focuses on executing the work).
  - Step 5: **EDIT FILTER** (focuses on high-volume drafting followed by structured refinement).
  - Step 6: **PUBLISH** (focuses on distribution).
  - Step 7: **UPDATE** (focuses on identifying and doubling down on successful content).
